About Us

We are a digital transformation consultancy and engineering company that delivers cutting edge solutions for global organisations and technology startups. Since 2007 we have been helping companies and established brands reimagine their business through digitalisation.

Why Choose HnG Consulting

HnG Consulting provides information technology staffing services for our clients both nationwide and internationally. Our main markets include Arizona, California, Colorado, Georgia, New Jersey, New York, Oregon, and Washington; however, we have contractors working throughout the US in over 20 different states.

HnG Consulting supplies Information Technology professionals, both technical and functional, to support our clients in the execution of their technology initiatives as well as with the maintenance of their existing IT systems, applications and infrastructure.

As an organization, HnG Consulting believes first and foremost in exceeding the expectations of those we serve. Our staff is committed to delivering outstanding results and our reputation is our top priority. We understand and appreciate that clients and contractors have many choices in the world of IT staffing, and we seek to deliver unparalleled customer service and contractor care to earn and keep their business and trust.
